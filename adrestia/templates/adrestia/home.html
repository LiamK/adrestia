Dear Superdelegate,

Let your Superdelegate know how you feel!

Here's all the information you need to contact your state's Democratic Superdelegates or the at-large ones. Telephone, Email, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube – even snail mail addresses.
You can find your Superdelegate here.

Many Superdelegates are very nice people doing their best in a system they didn't create. If you choose to contact them everyone will appreciate the respectful tone you use.

You can also see which Superdelegates are facing primary challenges.

It turns out there are quite a few "Bernie Candidates" out there. Some are incumbents and a good number are running for the first time. If you want to encourage them by making a donation they will surely appreciate it!

In some cases, the Superdelegates seem to be remarkably out of step with the voters in their state. There is a Superdelegate chart for each state which gives a graphic representation of the delegate and Superdelegate relationship.
